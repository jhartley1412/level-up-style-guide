Style Guide

Colors

All the colors for Level Up Learning were chosen with kids in mind. Since this, ideally, would be an app primarily used by kids, it needed to capture their attention and captivation.

Primary Colors

The colors most used throughout the app. These two colors are used for the structure colors (headers, buttons, etc). They bright to incite excitement and fun. The orange and yellow combo also represents gold, treasure and something of value (such as a youths education).

#F59F06

#FBCC33

Secondary Colors

These four are also mainly throughout the app, however these are each representative of a specific subject in school. Purple for Science, Green for History (or Social Studies), Blue for Math, and Pink for English.

Typography

Arimo

The typography in Level Up Learning had to be, most of all, easy to read. Arimo, the main body typeface was the top pick for achieving this. Arimo is clean, simple (but not boring!) and, best of all, works well for both instructional text, as well as button text.

Montserrat

Like Arimo, Montserrat is another typeface that's very easy to real. It's also more bolded than other typefaces, which tend to be more kid-friendly. Montserrat works best for headers and sub headers. Basically anything that labels a section does so in Montserrat.
